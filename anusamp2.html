Our Climate Journey

Our planet is experiencing unprecedented changes. The Earth's temperature has risen by 1.1°C since the late 19th century, largely due to human activities, including the burning of fossil fuels, deforestation, and industrial processes. The impact of this rise is visible across the globe—from the shrinking Arctic ice to more intense weather events like hurricanes, wildfires, and droughts.

The time to act is now. Scientists around the world warn that if global temperatures continue to rise at this rate, we could face catastrophic effects within the next few decades. From coral reef extinction to significant sea-level rises, the future of our planet depends on our actions today.

Join us in understanding the science behind climate change, and learn how you can contribute to reversing this trend. Together, we can make a difference.
